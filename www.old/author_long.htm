»  Priscilla Long

Books edited: The New Left: A Collection of Essays

Priscilla Long is a graduate of Moravian Seminary for Girls and Antioch College, where she was active in the Student Peace Union and started a coeducational cooperative dorm. She originally comes from Chestertown, MD. The New Left: A Collection of Essays is her first book.
